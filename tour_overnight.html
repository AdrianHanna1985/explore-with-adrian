🎒 Gear Recommendations & Checklist

  • Backpack (50L or larger)
  • Sleeping bag & sleeping pad
  • Lightweight tent or bivy
  • Layered clothing for warm days & cold nights
  • Camp stove or JetBoil (optional)
  • Headlamp, sunscreen, bug spray
  • 2L+ water capacity, filter or purification tablets
  • Bear-proof canister (provided)
