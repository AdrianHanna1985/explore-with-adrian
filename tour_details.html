Mount Whitney Summit Full-Day Tour

Climb the highest peak in the lower 48 in one epic day! Our expert guide supports your summit bid with pace, safety, and inspiration.

Key Facts

  • Distance: ~21.4 mi (11 mi one-way)
  • Elevation Gain: ~6,100 ft
  • Trailhead: Whitney Portal – 8,360 ft
  • Summit Elevation: 14,505 ft (4,421 m)
  • Difficulty: Strenuous / Highly Challenging
  • Season: June–September; early/late season may require winter gear
  • Permits: Lottery required between May–Nov (100 day, 60 overnight users daily)

Collapsible FAQ

❓ When do we start and return?

Typical start time is 10pm the night before. Return expected ~5pm following day.

❓ What gear do I need?

Headlamp, layers, gloves, trekking poles, snacks, 3+ liters water, electrolyte tablets.

❓ Are restrooms available?

Toilets at trailhead and Outpost Camp; WAG bags required above 10,000 ft.
