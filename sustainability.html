Our Commitment to Sustainability

At The American Backpacker , we believe that every adventure should help preserve the wild places we love. From minimizing impact on fragile ecosystems to supporting local conservation efforts, we integrate sustainable practices in every step of our journey.

What We're Doing

  • Leave No Trace ethics on all guided trips
  • Use of eco-friendly gear and reusable cookware
  • Partnering with local trail restoration programs
  • Carbon offset contributions from each trip booked
  • Support for Indigenous stewardship and cultural protection

How You Can Help

  • Pack in, pack out — even small trash like wrappers
  • Use refillable water bottles and avoid single-use plastics
  • Respect wildlife and never veer off-trail
  • Join our seasonal clean-up hikes
  • Consider our sustainability-focused membership tier
