Travertine Hot Springs: A Soak with a View

Posted on June 22, 2025 · By Adrian

Just outside Bridgeport, Travertine Hot Springs offers one of the most surreal geothermal experiences in the Eastern Sierra. Natural tubs, a view of the Sawtooth Range, and mineral-stained rocks made this an unforgettable morning soak.

It’s not just the warm water—it’s the silence, the morning steam rising as the sun hits the granite peaks, and the feeling of being both grounded and elevated. Few places deliver this kind of wild peace.

Getting here was easy with the gravel road just south of Bridgeport. Come early, bring water, and leave no trace. It’s a rare gem—let’s keep it that way.
